Energy Presentation : January 2013

Created as a mock-up presentation for an energy company. The prospective client was looking for slides that could capture the look and feel of clean, sustainable energy. Coming into a new and exciting market with many possibilities, I chose bright, vibrant colors. I overlaid graphs and information on high resolution pictures, a tribute to the classic idea of sustainability: reuse.
